Greek: λόγος (logos) — word, reason, account, principle, discourse
Philosophy: Heraclitus through Stoics, John "In the beginning was the Word"
Meaning: The articulation of cosmic order into speech and understanding
AFTER KOSMOS COMES LOGOS:

Heraclitus: λόγος τόνδε τοῦ κόσμου (this logos of the cosmos)
The word that holds the cosmos together. The articulation of order.
